USER: What does Lilian Weng say about types of reward hacking?
ASSISTANT: Lilian Weng categorises reward hacking into two types: environment or goal misspecification, and reward tampering. Environment or goal misspecification occurs when the agent exploits flaws in the environment or optimises a misaligned reward function rather than the true objective. Reward tampering involves the agent directly interfering with the reward mechanism itself.
