USER: How does FActScore evaluate LLM factuality?
ASSISTANT: FActScore decomposes long-form generation into atomic facts and validates each fact against a knowledge base. Retrieval-augmented validation significantly outperforms approaches that do not use retrieval. It was introduced by Min et al. (2023).
USER: Is that different from SelfCheckGPT?
ASSISTANT: Yes. SelfCheckGPT checks consistency by sampling multiple outputs from the model and comparing them — it does not require an external knowledge base. FActScore, by contrast, validates each atomic fact against a retrieved knowledge source.
